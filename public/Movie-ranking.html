Anime Series and Movie Review

My Favourite Anime of all time

Haikyuu

The story is about a short guy who wants to fly. He loves volleyball and he joins a fallen high-school volleyball team
Genre : arousing, sports, Motivational

Your name

This anime movie is about a girl and a guy who switched souls and also travelled through time
Genre : Romance, Emotional

I want to eat your pancreas

The name might be a bit weird, but this anime movie will make any grown man weep. Very emotional and heartbreaking. This movie is about a very jovial high school girl who was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer and she decides to spend her last days on Earth with an introverted guy whom she met at the hospital when she was diagnosed.
Genre : Emotional, Heart breaking
